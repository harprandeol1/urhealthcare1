A modern approach to primary care

This is not a typical doctor’s office. urHealthcare is a membership-based direct primary care practice designed around your family’s needs. Instead of billing insurance for every visit, you pay a simple monthly fee for unlimited access to your care team, urgent care, virtual visits, medications, and labs — with no surprise bills.
