FAQ

How does it work?

Unlimited visits with no copays or deductibles. Primary, urgent, basic imaging, labs, and many meds are included.

Do you handle referrals?

Yes—we coordinate referrals. Specialty/hospital services are outside the DPC fee; consider pairing with HDHP/catastrophic insurance.

Can you help me pick insurance?

Yes. We’ll guide you on pairing DPC with the right insurance.
