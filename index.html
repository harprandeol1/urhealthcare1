FAQs

How does membership work?

Unlimited visits with no copays or deductibles. Many meds, basic imaging, labs, and minor procedures are included in your monthly fee.

Do you handle referrals?

Yes. We coordinate specialist referrals. Specialty/hospital care is outside the DPC fee; we’ll help you pair with high-deductible or catastrophic coverage.

Can you help me choose insurance?

Yes. We’ll guide you on coupling DPC with insurance based on your needs.
