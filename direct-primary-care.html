What is Direct Primary Care?

Healthcare without the headaches—just a simple membership

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is a modern, patient-centered approach that removes insurance middlemen so you can focus on your health with your doctor. Instead of paying for each visit, you join through a monthly membership that covers unlimited access to primary and urgent care.

  • Membership model: Unlimited visits, extended appointments, same/next-day access, and direct messaging with your care team.
  • No insurance hassles: No copays, deductibles, or surprise bills.
  • Comprehensive care: Preventive, chronic, urgent care, many meds, basic labs, and minor procedures included.

Care that fits your life

In-person or virtual, seven days a week for urgent needs—so you get answers and treatment fast, without the runaround.

Specialty and hospital services are outside the DPC fee; many members pair DPC with a high-deductible or catastrophic plan.

Why It’s Different from Traditional Healthcare

In traditional healthcare, insurance companies often dictate how doctors practice—limiting time with patients and inflating costs. Direct Primary Care flips that model: your membership is a direct relationship with your doctor. This means your provider works for you—not the insurance company.

Direct Primary Care — FAQ

Is DPC the same as health insurance?

No. DPC covers primary and urgent care, many medications, basic labs, and minor procedures. Specialty and hospital services are outside the membership. Many members pair DPC with a high-deductible plan.

How do I sign up?

You can contact us or join directly online. Enrollment takes just a few minutes.
