What is Direct Primary Care?

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is a modern, patient-centered approach to healthcare that removes the middlemen—insurance companies, networks, and third-party approvals—so you can focus on your health with your doctor. Instead of paying for each visit or service, patients join through a simple monthly membership that covers unlimited access to primary and urgent care.

Our Membership Pricing

  • Individual: $79/month – Unlimited primary & urgent care, same/next-day access, and virtual visits.
  • Spouse: $50/month – Add your partner with full membership benefits.
  • Child (5+): $30/month – Pediatric sick & well visits with rapid access for families.

Specialty and hospital services are outside the DPC fee. Many members pair DPC with a high-deductible or catastrophic insurance plan for broader coverage.

Why It’s Different

In traditional healthcare, insurance companies often dictate how doctors practice—limiting time with patients and inflating costs. Direct Primary Care flips that model: your membership is a direct relationship with your doctor. This means your provider works for you—not for the insurance company.

Who Benefits from DPC?

  • Individuals & Families – Affordable, predictable healthcare costs with unlimited access.
  • Employers – Lower healthcare costs while providing better access and fewer sick days.
  • Patients with Chronic Conditions – Ongoing support without worrying about visit costs.

In short: Direct Primary Care at urHealthcare is simple, affordable, and built entirely around you. It’s healthcare the way it should be—transparent, convenient, and relationship-driven.
