Pair DPC with High-Deductible or Catastrophic Insurance

Combine your DPC membership with a high-deductible (HDHP) or catastrophic insurance plan to cover specialty, hospital, or emergency needs.

  • Lower total cost: Many members see premiums and overall healthcare costs go down by ~20–25% when pairing DPC with an HDHP.
  • Coverage for the rare stuff: About 95% of everyday care is primary care—we take care of that directly. Catastrophic/HDHP plans handle the bigger, rarer events.
  • We’ll guide you: Our team can walk you through options so your plan fits your health and budget.

Membership FAQ

Is DPC the same as health insurance?

No. DPC covers primary & urgent care, many medications, basic labs, and minor procedures. Specialty and hospital services are outside the membership. Most members pair DPC with an HDHP/catastrophic plan.

What’s included with my membership?

Unlimited visits, same/next-day access, 7-day urgent care, 250+ covered meds, basic labs, X-ray & ultrasound, and common procedures like stitches, splints, and abscess drainage.

Is DPC HSA-eligible? Do you bill insurance?

HSA: Expected to be eligible Jan 2026 (confirm with your tax advisor and plan administrator). We don’t bill insurance for membership; most members use insurance for specialty/hospital needs.

How fast can I be seen?

Same/next-day appointments are typical. We also offer virtual care and 7-day urgent care access.

Are meds and labs really included?

Yes—common meds on our covered list are included at no extra cost, along with routine labs and on-site X-ray/ultrasound for faster diagnosis.

Is there an age limit?

Membership is available to patients 65 years and younger. Children 5+ can be added.

What if I need a specialist or surgery?

We coordinate referrals and help manage care. Outside specialty, advanced imaging, hospital, or surgery are typically covered through your insurance plan.
