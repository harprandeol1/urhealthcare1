Direct Primary Care — FAQ

What is direct primary care (DPC)?

DPC is a membership model for comprehensive, relationship-based primary care. You pay a simple monthly fee for unlimited visits and direct access to your care team, with many services included.

Is DPC the same as health insurance?

No. DPC covers primary and urgent care, many medications, basic labs, and minor procedures. Specialty and hospital services are outside the membership. Many members pair DPC with a high-deductible
