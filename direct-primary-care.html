What is Direct Primary Care?

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is a modern, patient-centered approach that removes insurance middlemen so you can focus on your health with your doctor. Instead of paying for each visit, you join through a simple monthly membership that covers unlimited access to primary and urgent care.

Why It’s Different

In traditional healthcare, insurance companies often dictate how doctors practice—limiting time with patients and inflating costs. Direct Primary Care flips that model: your membership is a direct relationship with your doctor.

Pair DPC with High-Deductible or Catastrophic Insurance

We help you combine your DPC membership with a high-deductible (HDHP) or catastrophic insurance plan to cover specialty, hospital, or emergency needs.

  • Lower total cost: Many members see premiums and overall healthcare costs go down by ~20–25% when pairing DPC with an HDHP.
  • Coverage for the rare stuff: About 95% of everyday care is primary care—we take care of that directly. Catastrophic/HDHP plans handle the bigger, rarer events.
  • We’ll guide you: Our team can walk you through options so your plan fits your health and budget.

FAQ

Is DPC the same as health insurance?

No. DPC covers primary and urgent care, many medications, basic labs, and minor procedures. Specialty and hospital services are outside the membership. Most members pair DPC with a high-deductible or catastrophic insurance plan.

Are medications included?

Yes. Medications listed on our covered list are provided at no extra cost and included in the membership.

What if I need a specialist or advanced imaging?

We’ll coordinate referrals and help manage your care. Costs for outside specialty, advanced imaging, hospital, or surgery are typically covered through your traditional insurance plan and are not part of the DPC membership.

Is the DPC membership HSA-eligible?

Yes — effective January 2026, DPC membership will be HSA-eligible. (Confirm with your tax advisor and plan administrator; eligibility may vary by plan.)

Is there an age limit?

Yes. This membership is available to patients 65 years and younger.
