What is Direct Primary Care?

Direct Primary Care (DPC) removes insurance middlemen so you get a direct relationship with your clinician. Instead of paying per visit, you join with a simple monthly membership that covers unlimited primary and urgent care access.

Membership FAQ

Do you accept insurance with DPC?

Membership is separate from insurance and covers primary & urgent care at urHealthcare. Many members keep a high-deductible plan for outside services.

Is Partnership Medi-Cal accepted?

Yes, for non-membership patients we accept major plans including Partnership Medi-Cal. Many patients also choose DPC for predictable costs.

What meds and labs are included?

Over 250 common medications and basic labs are included. See our med list and lab menu.

Can employers enroll teams?

Yes—DPC reduces costs and improves access. See Employers for options.
