What is Direct Primary Care?

Direct Primary Care (DPC) is a modern, patient-centered approach that removes insurance middlemen so you can focus on your health with your doctor. Instead of paying for each visit, you join through a simple monthly membership that covers unlimited access to primary and urgent care.

Why It’s Different

In traditional healthcare, insurance companies often dictate how doctors practice—limiting time with patients and inflating costs. Direct Primary Care flips that model: your membership is a direct relationship with your doctor.
