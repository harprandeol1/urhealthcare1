FAQ

Is DPC the same as insurance?

No. DPC covers primary & urgent care (many meds/labs/procedures). Specialty, hospital, surgery, and ER are outside the membership and typically handled by insurance.

Do you offer telehealth and after-hours help?

Yes—same/next-day video visits and 7-day urgent care. After-hours messaging guides next steps for urgent issues.

Can kids join? What ages?

Yes, ages 5+. Pediatric sick & well visits are included. Vaccinations may be billed separately if obtained externally.

How do billing and cancellations work?

Choose monthly or yearly. Cancel anytime before your next billing cycle—no long-term contract.

Is the membership HSA/FSA eligible?

Yes—effective January 2026 DPC is HSA-eligible (confirm with your tax advisor/plan). FSA cards are commonly accepted for eligible services.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes—contact us to switch before your next billing date.

Are medications included?

Yes—our covered list is included at no extra cost for members.

Do you coordinate referrals and imaging?

Yes—we coordinate specialists and advanced imaging and help navigate in-network options.

Is there an age limit?

Membership is available for patients 65 years and younger.
