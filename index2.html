Membership FAQ

What is Direct Primary Care (DPC)?

DPC is a simple monthly membership for primary & urgent care. It removes middlemen and visit-by-visit billing so you can focus on your health with your clinician.

Do I need insurance to join?

No. DPC is separate from insurance. Many members keep a high-deductible or catastrophic plan for hospital/specialty care and use DPC for everyday care.

Are urgent care visits included?

Yes—urgent care is included with your membership (walk-in or virtual) for common illnesses and injuries.

What labs and medications are included?

Members get basic labs and rapid tests in clinic, plus access to 250+ commonly used medications included with membership.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Membership is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime before your next billing date.

Can I use HSA or FSA funds?

Many patients do. Check with your plan administrator for eligibility details for membership fees and services.
