Medi-Cal / Partnership HealthPlan — FAQ

Do you accept Partnership HealthPlan (PHC) Medi-Cal?

Yes. We accept Medi-Cal patients, including those enrolled with Partnership HealthPlan of California. Bring your insurance card and photo ID; we’ll verify benefits at check-in.

Can I switch my PCP to urHealthcare?

In most cases, yes. Use your plan’s member portal or call the number on your card to request urHealthcare as your PCP. We can guide you if you need help.

Are labs and urgent care covered?

Coverage depends on your plan. Many services are covered when medically necessary. We’ll confirm eligibility, obtain any required authorizations, and explain costs up front.

What if I don’t have Medi-Cal?

We accept many commercial plans and offer Direct Primary Care membership for transparent pricing.
