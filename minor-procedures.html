Minor Procedures FAQ

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins welcome. For complex cases, calling ahead can help us prepare.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. We accept most major insurances. See our insurance list. Copays/deductibles vary by plan.

How do you decide cash pricing?

Prices reflect procedure complexity, materials, and medications used. We provide an estimate before treatment and stay transparent.

Can you do X-rays, splints, and stitches in the same visit?

Yes, when appropriate. We offer on-site X-ray ($75 cash), splints, wound closure, and follow-up care.

What if I need a specialist or surgery?

We’ll stabilize you, provide initial treatment, and coordinate timely referrals to in-network specialists when needed.
