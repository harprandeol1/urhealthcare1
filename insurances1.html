Insurance FAQ

Do you accept HMO and PPO plans?

Yes. We work with many PPO and HMO plans listed above. Some HMO plans require choosing a primary care provider (PCP) or a referral for specialty care—our team will guide you.

What if my plan isn’t listed?

Call us. Many out-of-network plans still include out-of-network benefits or cash options. We also offer a Direct Primary Care membership for transparent pricing.

How do you verify coverage and costs?

We verify eligibility and benefits at check-in. Before treatment, we’ll explain any copays, deductibles, or coinsurance so there are no surprises.

Need coverage?

We can help you sign up for Medi-Cal, Medicare, or a private insurance plan that fits your needs.
