How Virtual Care Works

1) Book or Walk-In (Virtual)

Same-day availability. Call or request online. We’ll text/email a secure video link.

2) Video Visit

Meet with a provider on your phone or computer. We’ll diagnose and treat, order meds, and arrange tests if needed.

3) Care Plan & Follow-Up

We’ll send after-visit instructions, arrange labs/imaging, and coordinate referrals. Members get direct messaging for follow-up.

Tech Requirements

Any smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and microphone. Use Wi-Fi or strong cellular data. Enable notifications/location if prompted for nearby pharmacy lookup.

Virtual care is HIPAA-compliant. For emergencies (e.g., severe chest pain, difficulty breathing), call 911 or go to the nearest ER.
