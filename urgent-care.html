Urgent Care FAQ (Rocklin 95677)

Do you take walk-ins? How fast can I be seen?

Yes, we take walk-ins and also offer online scheduling. Same/next-day availability is typical—walk in or check in online.

Which symptoms should go to urgent care vs. the ER?

We treat most minor to moderate illnesses and injuries. Call 911 or go to the ER for severe chest pain, stroke symptoms, major trauma, severe breathing trouble, or uncontrolled bleeding.

Is X-ray, COVID/flu testing, and labs available on-site?

Yes. We perform X-rays, rapid tests (flu, strep, COVID-19), pregnancy & UTI testing, and basic labs in-house. We can also draw samples and send out when necessary.

Do you accept Partnership Medi-Cal and other insurance?

Yes, we accept major health plans including Partnership Medi-Cal. View details on our Accepted Insurances page or call us to confirm your specific plan.

Do you offer a membership if I don’t want to use insurance?

Yes. Our Direct Primary Care membership offers unlimited primary & urgent care visits for a flat monthly price.
