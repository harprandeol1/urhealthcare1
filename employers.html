Employer FAQ

How does DPC reduce our healthcare costs?

We offload 95% of everyday needs into unlimited, rapid-access primary care — reducing ER/urgent visits, avoidable specialty, and time away from work. We then pair (“club”) your plan with a high-deductible or catastrophic option for big, rare events.

Do you help us combine DPC with an HDHP?

Yes. We’ll help select and implement the right HDHP/catastrophic plan so employees keep great access while you trim premiums.

Will this impact workers’ compensation?

Earlier access often means faster recovery and fewer escalations, contributing to lower overall workers’ comp costs.

Can you support self-funded employers?

Absolutely — proactive care, included meds/labs, and on-site diagnostics reduce high-cost claims. Savings can be substantial for self-funded groups.

What’s the guarantee?

If we don’t lower your healthcare premiums, we guarantee 50% money back on urHealthcare membership fees for that plan year.

Any trials for larger employers?

Yes. Employers with 50+ employees receive one month free to try us out.

How do we manage employees?

All employees can be managed in a single portal — simple enrollments, roster syncs, and usage insights. Plans can be tailored by location, role, or population needs.
