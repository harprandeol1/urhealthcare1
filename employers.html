Employer FAQ

How does DPC reduce our healthcare costs?

We offload ~95% of everyday needs into rapid-access primary care — reducing ER/urgent visits, avoidable specialty, and time away from work.

What savings should we expect?

Many employers see 20–25% lower total spend when pairing DPC with an HDHP, depending on current plan design and utilization.

What’s the guarantee?

If we don’t lower your healthcare premiums, we guarantee 50% money back on urHealthcare membership fees for that plan year.

Is DPC insurance?

No. DPC covers primary/urgent care, many meds, basic labs & procedures. Specialty/hospital care runs through your HDHP/insurer.

Can you help select an HDHP?

Yes — we help select & implement an HDHP/catastrophic plan that pairs with DPC, keeping access high while trimming premiums.

Self-funded groups?

Yes. Proactive care, included meds/labs, and on-site diagnostics reduce high-cost claims and downstream spend.

How do we manage employees?

Single employer portal: roster, enrollments, usage & outcomes. Plans can be tailored by role, population, or location.

Impact on workers’ compensation?

Earlier access → faster recovery, fewer escalations, and typically lower overall workers’ comp costs.

Implementation timeline?

Most groups can go live within a few weeks depending on carrier and payroll coordination.

Employee experience

Same/next-day appointments, 7-day urgent care, virtual care, included meds & labs, and 24/7 care manager.

How do employees get help after hours?

24/7 care navigation & messaging for guidance and next steps.

Any trials for larger employers?

Yes — employers with 50+ employees get one month free to try us.
