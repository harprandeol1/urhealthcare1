Partnership Medi-Cal FAQ — Placer & Sacramento County

Do you accept Partnership Medi-Cal?

Yes—we accept Partnership Medi-Cal. Bring your plan ID card and a photo ID; we’ll verify benefits at check-in.

How do I choose urHealthcare as my PCP?

Call the member services number on your card and request urHealthcare — Rocklin (95677) as your primary care clinic. We can help with details in-clinic.

Is urgent care covered?

Many urgent care services are covered. Plan rules vary; we’ll check benefits and any copays/authorizations before treatment.

Do you have in-house labs and X-ray?

Yes—common labs and rapid tests are done on-site, with X-ray and minor procedures, to speed your diagnosis and care plan.

Do you offer transportation help?

We assist eligible members in coordinating rides using plan benefits and local partners. Ask when scheduling.

Can I use DPC along with my plan?

Yes—many patients choose our DPC membership for transparent pricing and access, while keeping insurance for specialty/hospital care.
