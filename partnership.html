Partnership Medi-Cal FAQ (Rocklin 95677)

Do you accept Partnership Medi-Cal?

Yes. We accept Partnership Medi-Cal. Bring your plan ID card and a photo ID to your visit, and we’ll verify benefits at check-in.

How do I choose urHealthcare as my PCP?

Call the member services number on the back of your Partnership Medi-Cal card and request urHealthcare in Rocklin as your primary care clinic. We can assist with details at the front desk.

Is urgent care covered?

Many urgent care services are covered. We’ll check your specific benefits and let you know about any copays/authorizations before treatment.

Do you have in-house labs and X-ray?

Yes. We perform many labs and rapid tests on-site and provide X-ray and basic procedures, which helps you get treated sooner without extra trips.

Do you offer transportation assistance?

We can help coordinate rides for eligible members using plan benefits and local partners. Ask our team when you schedule.

Can I use Direct Primary Care (DPC) with my plan?

Yes. Many members choose DPC for transparent pricing and quicker access while keeping insurance for specialty and hospital care. We’ll help you pair DPC with your benefits.
